ODPC registration, lawful basis, data subject rights

Formation, consideration, capacity, illegality

UNCITRAL Model Law adopted, NY Convention ratified, s.6 mandatory stay

Incorporation, directors, shares, winding up

Wills, intestacy, probate, letters of administration

Procedural law — key rules
Civil Procedure Rules 2010 (Or. 1–51)
Or.26 injunctions, Or.53 judicial review, Or.23 garnishee, Or.9/10 judgment
Limitation of Actions Act (Cap. 22)
6 yrs contract · 3 yrs tort · 12 yrs land — cannot be shortened by contract
Exclusive jurisdiction — ELRC & ELC
Employment → ELRC only; Land/environment → ELC only; clauses ousting this are void
Stamp Duty Act (Cap. 480)
Conveyances 4%, leases, share transfers 1%, debentures — instruments must be stamped
Court of Appeal Rules 2010
30 days notice of appeal (r.75); record of appeal within 60 days
Small Claims Court Act 2016
Claims ≤ KES 1,000,000 · No advocates · 60-day target
Common risk areas for Kenyan contracts
Choice of foreign law clauses
Courts may refuse to apply if contrary to Kenyan public policy (Constitution Art. 2(4)) — Hashi Energy v KRA [2019]
Ouster of court jurisdiction
Any clause excluding Kenyan courts entirely is void — Art. 48 CoK (access to justice) unless valid arbitration clause
Shortened limitation periods
Cannot contractually shorten below Limitation of Actions Act (Cap. 22) — Gabriel Mutava v KPA [2016]
Cross-border data transfers
Data Protection Act s.48 requires adequate protection or explicit consent
Penalty clauses
Must represent genuine pre-estimate of loss; unconscionable penalties void under Kenyan equity
Consumer protection
Consumer Protection Act 2012 — unfair terms unenforceable in B2C contracts
Pathological arbitration clauses
No seat, contradictory rules, no governing law — clause may be unenforceable; Esso (Kenya) Ltd v MV "Saruji" [1994]
